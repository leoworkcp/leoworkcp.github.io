About

Hi! My name is Leo; I’m a software engineer. Since I was a young child I developed an interest in computers, technology, and the expansion of modern software. I became curious about how these machines operated and how the impact of the internet further advanced this industry. My passion grew more when I went into college for a computer science BA degreed and since them i’ve become a full-stack Software Engineer with experience in Python, JavaScript, C#, React-Redux and many other technologies. My interests in programming are rooted in a desire to understand the inner working of the “black box”, by breaking down abstract concepts into their functional components. I’m looking for an environment that is fast paced with opportunities to continue learning and growth.
